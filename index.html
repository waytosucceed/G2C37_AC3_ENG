Comparative and Superlative Adjectives

ðŸ“š Comparative and superlative adjectives help us compare things around us.

Comparative adjectives are words that help us compare two things, showing how one is different from the other. We usually add -er to the end of the adjective when we compare.

Superlative adjectives are special words we use when we want to say something is the most of all. We usually add -est to the end of the adjective to show this.
